World History Figures

Nelson Mandela

🌍 Fought against apartheid in South Africa and became the country's first Black president.

Abraham Lincoln

Ended slavery in the U.S. and led the country during the Civil War.

Winston Churchill

British Prime Minister during World War II known for inspiring leadership.

Philippine History Figures

José Rizal

✍️ National hero who used writing to inspire Filipino nationalism.

Andrés Bonifacio

⚔️ Leader of the Katipunan who fought for independence from Spain.

Emilio Aguinaldo

First President of the Philippines and leader in the revolution.

Islamic History Figures

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

🕋 Founder of Islam and messenger of the Quran.

Saladin

⚔️ Muslim leader who recaptured Jerusalem during the Crusades with honor and respect.

Avicenna (Ibn Sina)

📚 Muslim scholar who wrote about medicine, science, and philosophy during the Islamic Golden Age.
