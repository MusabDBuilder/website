Famous Scientists and Their Contributions

1. Albert Einstein (1879–1955)

🧠 Developed the Theory of Relativity, which explains how time and space are connected.

2. Isaac Newton (1643–1727)

🌍 Discovered the laws of motion and gravity, which describe how objects move.

3. Marie Curie (1867–1934)

⚛️ Pioneered research on radioactivity. First woman to win a Nobel Prize — and she won it twice!

4. Galileo Galilei (1564–1642)

🔭 Improved the telescope and helped prove that the Earth moves around the Sun.

5. Charles Darwin (1809–1882)

🌱 Known for the theory of evolution by natural selection. Wrote The Origin of Species.

6. Nikola Tesla (1856–1943)

⚡ Worked on electricity and helped develop the alternating current (AC) system we use today.

7. Rosalind Franklin (1920–1958)

🧬 Her work with X-ray images helped reveal the structure of DNA.

8. Stephen Hawking (1942–2018)

🌌 Studied black holes and the origin of the universe. Wrote A Brief History of Time.
