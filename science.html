What is Science?

Science is the study of the natural world through **observation, experiments, and evidence-based reasoning**. It helps us understand how things work, from tiny atoms to the vast universe.

Science is divided into different branches:

  • Physics – The study of forces, motion, energy, and matter.
  • Chemistry – The study of substances, their properties, and how they interact.
  • Biology – The study of living organisms, from bacteria to humans.
  • Earth Science – The study of Earth's structure, climate, and space.

Biology

✔ Cells – The building blocks of life

✔ Photosynthesis – How plants make their own food

✔ Human Body Systems – Heart, brain, lungs, and more

Earth Science

✔ The Solar System – Planets, moons, and the Sun

✔ Weather and Climate – What makes rain and storms

✔ Rocks and Minerals – What Earth is made of

Interesting Science Facts

🚀 The **speed of light** is about 299,792,458 meters per second!

🌱 Plants **breathe** through tiny holes in their leaves called **stomata**.

🔬 Water can boil and freeze at the same time in a process called **triple point**.

🌍 The Earth's core is as hot as the surface of the **Sun** (~5,500°C).

🧬 You share **about 60% of your DNA** with bananas! 🍌
