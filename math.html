What is Math?

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, patterns, and logical reasoning. It helps us understand and solve real-world problems by using calculations, measurements, and formulas.

Math is used everywhere in daily life, including shopping (calculating prices), sports (keeping scores), science (measuring distances), and technology (programming and design).

Math is divided into different branches:

  • Arithmetic – Basic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Algebra – Using symbols and letters to represent numbers in equations.
  • Geometry – Studying shapes, sizes, and angles.
  • Statistics – Collecting and analyzing data to make conclusions.
  • Trigonometry – Studying angles and triangles.
